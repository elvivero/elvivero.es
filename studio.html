El vivero design studio began as the partnership between Leandro Lattes and Florencia Grassi in 2004. Based in Madrid, we collaborate with major cultural, art, science and tourism organizations, as well as many other clients in Spain and abroad.

We like design with a sense of humor that playfully includes everyday references whenever appropriate; design with visual content rigorously thought out to insure its coherence and thereby withstand the test of time.

We collaborate closely with our clients to clearly define the objectives, contents and special characteristics of every project. We shepherd each and every phase, from conception to implementation. Our conviction is that projects flourish when carried out with close attention and true dedication.
